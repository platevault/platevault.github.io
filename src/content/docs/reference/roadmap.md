The Manual and How-to pages describe the shipped product; this page
describes what comes next, with no dates and no ordering promises.

## Inbox

- **Framing attribution at confirm** — the confirm step will show ranked
  target-framing candidates and the attribution it chose, so you can see
  why files were grouped under a target.
- **Post-apply result link** — after a plan applies, a direct link will
  jump from the Inbox to the moved items in Sessions.

## Sessions & equipment

- **Equipment-driven resolution** — registered cameras and telescopes will
  drive the rest of the app: sessions resolve their camera through
  equipment aliases, and calibration matching shows equipment names
  instead of raw fingerprint strings.

## Targets & planner

- **Session linkage in the planner** — the planner will show, per target,
  the sessions already captured for it.
- **Richer planning astronomy** — twilight bands, Moon data, and
  constellation context in target planning and recommendations.

## Projects

- **One project-creation flow** — the quick-create dialog and the step
  wizard converge into a single creation experience.
- **Bidirectional notes sync** — edits made to note files outside the app
  will be detected and reflected.

## Cleanup & archive

- **Restore plans** — a reviewable plan generated from any archive entry
  that moves the archived files back into the library.
- **Archivable masters** — superseded or aged-out calibration masters get
  the same archive path projects have.
- **Free-space estimate** — cleanup plan review will show the
  destination's available space before you approve.

## Calibration

- **Un-assign a master** — return a session to "no master assigned" for a
  calibration type, instead of only replacing one master with another.

## Updater & distribution

- **Staged updates** — download and verify an update immediately, restart
  and install when you choose.
