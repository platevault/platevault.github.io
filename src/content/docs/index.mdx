## What PlateVault is

PlateVault is a desktop application that organizes an astrophotography
library: acquisition sessions, calibration masters, targets, processing
projects, and the cleanup and archive decisions that keep a large library
manageable. It indexes your files where they already live, maps the
relationships between them, prepares tool-friendly project inputs, and plans
filesystem work for your review.

PlateVault never processes images. Calibration, debayering, registration,
stacking, and editing belong to PixInsight/WBPP and comparable tools.
PlateVault organizes, maps, prepares, audits, and plans filesystem work —
nothing more.

## Three promises

1. **Local-first file custody.** Your image files stay where they are, on
   your disks, in your folder structure. PlateVault stores metadata,
   relationships, and decisions in its own database and never copies your
   library into a private store. Library roots are tracked separately from
   relative paths, so a moved drive can be remapped without rewriting
   history.
2. **Reviewable filesystem mutation.** Every move, copy, archive, or delete
   is proposed as a plan first. Only approving and applying a plan touches
   disk, nothing is overwritten silently, and destructive operations prefer
   archive or trash over permanent deletion.
3. **A durable audit trail.** Every applied action — and every refused or
   failed one — is recorded with its outcome and actor, so you can always
   reconstruct what happened to your library.

## Supported platforms

| Platform | Support |
| --- | --- |
| Windows x64 | Supported — NSIS installer or MSI |
| Linux x64 | Supported — AppImage, deb, or rpm |
| macOS Apple Silicon | Supported — dmg |
| macOS Intel | Not supported |

## Where to start

- Fresh machine: [Install PlateVault](how-to/install/), then let the
  [setup wizard](manual/setup-wizard/) register your folders.
- First night of data: [Ingest your first session](how-to/ingest-first-session/).
- Library that is already organized on disk:
  [Organize an existing messy library](how-to/organize-existing-library/).
- Ready to process: [Prepare inputs for PixInsight/WBPP](how-to/prepare-for-pixinsight/).
- Running out of disk: [Plan a cleanup safely](how-to/plan-a-cleanup/).

The Manual describes each page of the application; the How-to guides walk
through complete tasks end to end.
